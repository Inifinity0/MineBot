Mine-Bot

Chat-bot from Ministry of coal

A web-based chatbot that will serve as a platform for the citizens to get answer to their queries pertaining to various acts, rules and regulations applicable to the Ministry of Mining  of Bharat. A multilingual web-based platform, based on different regional/local languages which serves the people in terms of legal advices. Providing information about the laws and regulation that have been made by our government according to their needs. Offer convenient self service options and ensure more consistent support to the user. We provide the user proof of the acts and regulation under which parts of the laws we are proving them solutions.
